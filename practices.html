Comprehensive Legal Services

Focused · Precise · Comprehensive

Deep expertise in Ontario law across six core practice areas covering every aspect of your life and business.

Our team has extensive hands-on experience across the following areas. Whether you need personal legal advice or corporate counsel, we deliver professional, precise solutions tailored to your situation.

01

Civil & Commercial Litigation

We handle contract disputes, debt recovery, real estate litigation, equity disputes, partnership conflicts, and more. From case evaluation and evidence gathering through trial and arbitration, we pursue the best possible outcome for every client.

02

Criminal Defense

From investigation through appeal, we provide full-scope criminal defense. Services include bail hearings, motions to exclude evidence, plea negotiations, and trial advocacy, protecting your Charter rights at every stage.

03

Corporate Law

Full-cycle corporate legal services from startup to IPO: company formation and structuring, equity incentive plans, commercial contract review and drafting, M&A due diligence, compliance programs, and ongoing general counsel.

04

Family Law

Family matters handled with empathy and expertise: divorce proceedings, property division and valuation, child custody and access, spousal support, prenuptial agreements, estate planning, and probate. We minimize conflict and seek fair resolutions.

05

Intellectual Property

Protecting your innovation and brand value: Canadian trademark registration and enforcement, patent strategy, copyright registration, trade secret protection, IP licensing, and infringement litigation. Full-spectrum IP services for technology companies.

06

Employment Law

Employment law services for both employers and employees: employment contract drafting, wrongful dismissal claims, employment standards disputes, workers' compensation, non-compete clauses, and collective bargaining. Deep knowledge of the ESA and OHSA.
